My Thoughts...



“Try not to become a man of success, but rather try to become a man of value.” Albert Einstein

Friends, I, a common man can change and develop one and all, if you listen to my words; and think, self-analyse and experiment in your life with the understanding and insights hidden behind my words. Friends, after joining govt. service in the Indian Navy, I felt a deep inner-motivation to do something for development of people, and with the same motivation; I started writing books.

And, from a few days, my being or soul (whatever you name it), started inducing and compelling me to do something “differently” in this regard, and therefore now I have started making YouTube videos and speaking on ‘Podcast’ with a channel name – “Yes! We Can Change India”, to fulfill my same aim and ambition. And, I think in my whole life, including my future lives also, even I reach a little closer to this big aim; that is, development of one and all; it will be a great pleasure and satisfaction for me


The Existence is Our Real Creator

Friends, we all are children of the same womb of the creator-existence. The creator-existence has transformed chemical substances into a cell and this cell has been sprouted and developed in the form of a living world, and humanity is a part of this living world. Though, some religious and spiritual people believe that we have been created by a God— an unknown body; it is only a belief and not a proven reality. Reality, as proven by science is only this that we are all products of the nature and existence.

We have been created and developed on this earth after a long process of action and reaction, mutation and evolution. So, we are all children of the same nature and existence. Furthermore, though, among ourselves, superficially we may have some differences like different gender, colors and shapes; or our own created differences like caste, tradition, religion, nationality etc., however, we are all children of the same creator. Additionally, we should be aware that though humanity has been developed to the modern science age, observing the vastness and harsh conditions of the universe, this beautiful blue-planet earth seems as a womb (a safe place) for us.

Even today, we are not in a position to leave this planet and go to some other planet. In fact, modern science has not found any other planet in our whole galaxy or nearby galaxy, which is more beautiful and safer than our mother earth. Therefore, even today, we are like babies in the womb of the creator.

And Yes! We Are Chaning India.
