My Thoughts...



Leave Past and Utilize Present for betterGrowth and Development

Friends, people have this belief that we face problems and difficulties in our present life due to our past-life karma, in fact, past-life bad karma. Certainly, it is true that action-reaction and cause and effect always go together, but it is not true that creator-existence punishes us in our present life for our past-life karma. In fact, this existence is so kind and compassionate that it can bear any wound without hurting us.

riends, we should be aware that like a mother produces her baby to love, care and development; the creator-existence has produced us for our growth and development and not for punishing us. Notwithstanding, even this existence finds someone in an unhealthy state— ignorant, egoist, greedy, angered and violent; instead of punishing him; it puts its best effort to make him healthy, sensible, intelligent and developed. Because, even an unhealthy person is like a cell of the creator’s huge body, so there is no question of punishing its own cell. Therefore, this compassionate and awaken existence always cares us and it makes a sincere effort to make us healthy, developed and virtuous. However, due to ignorance and false spiritual and religious belief-system, we have created this false belief that God punishes us for our past karmas.


The Existence Is Purely Fair and Lovely

Friends, the facts and findings of modern science prove that nature is purely fair and crystal-clear. How, I explain here— the elementary particles of the existence like atoms and electrons are purely fair and unbiased. For example, everyone is same for an electron. It does not think—who is who or what. One may be a top most scientist, but an electron does not care; because it gives the same electric-shock to him what it gives to an illiterate. It does not differentiate between them. This clearly shows that nature and existence are purely fair and unbiased. In fact, this is the reason why in the spirituality it is said that everyone is same in the ‘eye’ of God; and that God is this existence. In addition, we should be aware that the intrinsic virtue of elementary particles does not change with time. An electron or an atom does not change with time, they are always same. It shows that this existence is fair and unbiased from the eternity.

WFurther, it is said that time is a great pain-balm. Time can heal our every wound. However, this is not the actual reality. The actual reality is that it is not the time; rather it is the healing-virtue of the nature (elementary particles) which heals our every wound. If there will be no healing-virtue in the nature, our wounds can never be healed. This healing-virtue of nature clearly shows that the existence is very loving and caring. It loves and cares us like a mother. In fact, the basic aim of the creator behind our creation is to care and develop us, so that we may be able to emerge out our in-built virtue, talent and intelligence; which the existence has been developing within us since eternity.

And Yes! We Are Chaning India.
